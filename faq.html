FAQs

What services does Prumac Connect offer?
We provide end-to-end logistics solutions including warehousing, inventory handling, nationwide distribution, last-mile delivery coordination, and supply chain support tailored to your business needs.
Which areas do you deliver to?
We offer nationwide delivery coverage. If you share your pickup and drop-off locations, we’ll confirm timelines and the best routing option for your shipment.
How do I request a quote?
You can request a quote via our website “Request a Quote” form or by contacting our team directly. Provide pickup/drop-off locations, cargo type, estimated weight/volume, and preferred delivery date for the fastest response.
Do you provide warehousing and storage?
Yes. We offer secure warehousing solutions for short-term and long-term storage, including organized inventory handling to help you manage stock efficiently.
How do you ensure shipments are safe and arrive on time?
We use standardized handling procedures, careful loading practices, and planned routing to reduce delays and protect cargo. Our team prioritizes clear communication throughout the delivery process.
Can you handle bulk deliveries and recurring distribution for businesses?
Absolutely. We support scheduled and recurring distribution, multi-stop routes, and bulk deliveries for businesses that need consistent nationwide movement of goods.
What information should I prepare before booking a delivery?
Please have the pickup and drop-off addresses, item description, packaging type, weight/size estimates, number of packages, special handling needs, and your preferred delivery window.
How can I track or get updates on my delivery?
Once your shipment is booked, our team provides progress updates and delivery confirmation. If you need an update at any time, contact support with your reference details and we’ll assist immediately.
